Breakfast Sando

A quick and easy (spicy) breakfast sandwich!

This recipe is perfect for weekend mornings. Not your boring egg sandwich.

I recommend you buy bagels at your local bagel shop. Grocery store bagels are tiny and boring. Get yourself big, artisan bagels that are freshly made with love and good ingredients. The bagel makes this recipe. Do not skimp on the bagel.

I like to use jalapeno cheddar bagels and mortadella, consider that the perfect bagel. Once you get this recipe down, feel free to explore.

Ingredients:

  • Jalapeno cheddar bagel, sliced and toasted
  • Mortadella, sliced (2 slices)
  • American Cheese (1 slice)
  • Mayonaise (1 tbsp)
  • Trader Joe's Habanero sauce (1 tsp or to taste)
  • Eggs (2)
  • Grass-fed Butter (1 tbsp)

Steps:

Making the eggs:
  1. In a small non-stick pan, heat the butter on medium-low heat. This low heat should cook the eggs slowly. Goal is to avoid burning the eggs.
  2. When butter is hot and just starting to smoke, add eggs. Do not break the yolk or stir just yet.
  3. Let eggs come up to temperature, egg white should turn mostly solid white.
  4. Break egg yolk with small silicone spatula, and mix lightly. Just enough to spread the yolk, but there should be clear egg white and yolk separation.
  5. Using the spatula, push the egg in from the pan edge towards the center, going around the pan edges. This creates an egg patty, with the top still wet and undercooked.
  6. Using the spatula, or a flip technique, carefully flip the egg patty.
  7. Turn off and remove from heat. Hot pan will finish cooking the egg.
Assembly:
  1. On bottom half of bagel, spread mayonaise and habanero sauce to combine.
  2. Place folded mortadella slices over top half of bagel.
  3. Place cheese slice on top of mortadella.
  4. Place egg patty on top of cheese.
  5. Combine bagel halves. Cut in half.
  6. Enjoy!
