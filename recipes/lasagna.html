Lasagna

A simple lasagna recipe that cooks in less than an hour!

Just kidding, this recipe takes time, but is so,so worth the effort.

This recipe uses canned tomato sauce to take advantage of the slow and ardous task of making a rich sauce, except someone else spent the time and energy doing it. Still, we use herbs and spices to give it a homemade taste. And the secret ingredient is sugar; never underestimate the power of acid.

No good sauce is ever made without the use of two proteins. Discover why sweet italian sausage and fatty ground beef make all the difference in this recipe. Give it a try and let me know what you think by sending me an email.

Ingredients:

  • Olive Oil (1 tbsp)
  • One can of good pasta sauce
  • Parmesan, grated (2 cups, divided)
  • Crushed Tomatoes (48 oz)
  • One pack of oven-ready lasagana noodles
  • Cottage cheese (24 oz)
  • Egg (1)
  • Parsley, chopped (1/4 cup)
  • Sliced Mozzerella (1 lb)
  • Sweet Italian Sausage (1lb)
  • Ground Beef (85%, 2lb)
  • Bell Pepper, julienned then diced (2)
  • Onion, diced (1)
  • Mushrooms, rinsed, chopped (8 oz)
  • Tomato Paste (1 tbsp)
  • Oregano (1 tsp)
  • Thyme (1 tsp)
  • Sugar (1 tbsp)
  • Salt (1 tsp)
  • Garlic, chopped (2 cloves)
  • White Wine (1 cup)

Steps:

Making the sauce:
  1. Over medium heat, start by browning the meat in a large pot.
  2. Drain meat of fat and place in a dish; set aside.
  3. Add olive oil, swirl to coat.
  4. Add garlic, and cook until aromatic (about 1 minute).
  5. Add bell pepper, mushroom and onion. Cook until tender.
  6. Add white wine. Reduce to half, about 3-5 minutes.
  7. Add tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes and paste.
  8. Add spices.
  9. Bring sauce to a simmer, then add meat back into pot.
  10. Allow sauce to simmer for 45 minutes
Making the cheesy filling:
  1. In a plastic bowl combine the egg, parmesan, cottage cheese and parsley.
  2. Combine until thoroughly mixed.
  3. Set aside.
Assembling and baking:
  1. Set oven to 425F.
  2. In a baking dish, layer the noodles with some overlap to prevent filling from leaking.
  3. Cover with mozzarella slices
  4. Spread a thin layer of cheese filling.
  5. Spread a thick layer of meat sauce.
  6. Repeat for second layer
  7. Finish off with parmesan on the top.
  8. Bake in the center rack for 40-45 mins until the top is brown and bubbly.
  9. Once finished, set aside and let cool for 30 mins.
  10. Enjoy!
