Pumpkin Muffins

My grandpa's favorite pumpkin flavored muffins!

This recipe is perfect for fall. Makes 48 regular muffins. Perfect for sharing with friends, family or coworkers.

Great for those who like a subtle pumpking flavor. This recipe will get you compliments every time!

Ingredients:

  • Crisco (1 cup)
  • Sugar (4 cups)
  • Cinnamon, ground (1.5 tsp)
  • Cloves, ground (1.5 tsp)
  • Vanilla (2 tsp)
  • Libby's Pure Pumpkin (1 large can)
  • Flour (5 cups)
  • Baking Soda (4 tsp)
  • Salt (1 tsp)
  • Milk chocolate chips (1 12 oz bag)
  • Paper or silicon cupcake/muffin cups (48)

Steps:

Making the batter:
  1. Mix flour, salt, and baking soda. Mix thoroughly.
  2. Mix spices, crisco, pumpkin, sugar and vanilla using stand mixer on low with paddle mixer attachment. Scraping sides occasionally.
  3. Slowly pour dry mixture into wet mixture, on low with paddle. Scraping sides occasionally.
  4. Add chocolate chips, on low with paddle. Scraping sides occasionally.
Assembling and baking:
  1. Set oven to 375F.
  2. Line cupcake pans with paper or silicone cups.
  3. Fill with batter 3/4 of the way.
  4. Bake for 24 mins, until tops are browned. Use fork or toothpick to ensure they are cooked thoroughly. No batter sticks to toothpick/fork.
  5. Once finished, set aside and let cool for 10 mins.
  6. Enjoy!
